About Us


At Eli Bonner LLC, we are your trusted partner for a wide range of essential services, ensuring that your home or business runs smoothly and efficiently. With a dedication to the trades as well as to excellence and a commitment to customer service, we have made a name in plumbing, electrical, water treatment, gas fitting, and more.


Our mission at Eli Bonner LLC is to provide top-quality services that improve the comfort, safety, and functionality of your living or working space. We believe that every project, whether big or small, deserves the utmost attention to detail, professionalism, and reliability.


Our current service areas include: Boyce, Berryville, White Post, Stephen's City, Winchester, Stephenson, Clear Brook, Kernstown, and Middletown.

Services

Plumbing

Our plumbing services cover a wide range of needs, from fixing leaky faucets to installing new plumbing systems.

Electrical

From fault finding, breakers, feeders, outlets, switches, generator installation and maintenance, and service panel upgrades.

Water well & water line servicing

No water or low water pressure situations. Well pump installation, pressure and storage tanks, filters, booster pumps, and VFDs.

Water treatment installation & servicing

We offer a range of water treatment solutions to meet your specific needs, from water testing to whole-house filtration systems.

Gas fitting installation & servicing

Our gas fitting services include installation of natural gas piping, propane lines and tanks, and leak detection.

Water heater installation & servicing

Our experienced plumbers can diagnose and fix any issue with your water heater, including leaks, faulty thermostats, and heating element problems.
